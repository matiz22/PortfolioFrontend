Who I am

I’m Mateusz — a student and active freelancer who enjoys turning messy, complex problems into simple, maintainable solutions. I love working directly with clients and aim to grow into full-time freelancing. My work focuses on clarity, reliability and pragmatic delivery.

How I choose tools

I don’t chase buzzwords — I pick the right tool for the problem. That means choosing solutions that match the scope, timeline and future maintainability of a project rather than following trends. My priority is practical, robust outputs that make a client’s life easier.

Practically, that looks like pragmatic architecture decisions, sensible trade-offs between speed and scalability, and a focus on code that’s easy to iterate on. I adapt my approach to fit mobile, web or backend needs depending on what the project requires.

For this portfolio I used Angular as I shifted some focus toward web development after working more on mobile and backend projects.

I’ve also completed a couple of projects in WordPress and can work across different stacks to deliver the right outcome.

My approach

Simple principle: make complex things simple. I break problems down, remove the unnecessary, and deliver pragmatic solutions that are easy to maintain. I value clear communication with clients, iterative delivery, and practical design decisions that keep projects on time and on budget.
